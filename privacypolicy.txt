UltraCalendar Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

INTRODUCTION

UltraCalendar is developed by the UltraCalendar team and is committed to protecting your privacy.

INFORMATION WE ACCESS

With permission, the app accesses:
- Google Calendar events (titles, times, locations, descriptions)
- Google Tasks and task lists
- Google Contacts birthday dates only

The App requests only the minimum permissions necessary to provide calendar sync functionality.

DATA STORAGE

All data retrieved from your Google account is stored locally on your device only. Calendar information remains in a local database and is never sent to external servers.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

UltraCalendar does not share, sell, or transmit your personal calendar data to any third-party services or servers. The app uses Google AdMob to display non-personalized advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID) for ad serving purposes. No calendar, task, or contact data is shared with advertisers.

AUTHENTICATION

Google account authentication is handled entirely by Google's official sign-in service. The app never accesses or stores your password; authentication tokens are managed by Android.

ANALYTICS AND TRACKING

UltraCalendar does not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, crash reporting tools, or any other form of usage monitoring.

ADVERTISING

The free version of UltraCalendar displays advertisements powered by Google AdMob. Ads appear as native cards within the calendar interface. Users may dismiss ads temporarily. Premium users who purchase the customization upgrade enjoy an ad-free experience. The app uses the Android Advertising ID for ad serving. No personal calendar data is used for ad targeting.

DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

Uninstalling removes all associated data. Users can also clear app data through device Settings.

REVOKING ACCESS

Access can be revoked at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

UltraCalendar is not directed at children under the age of 13.
