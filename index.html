UltraCalendar

A beautiful Material 3 calendar app for Android with home screen widgets

About

UltraCalendar syncs with your Google Calendar, Tasks, and Contacts to display events, tasks, and birthdays in a clean, modern interface. Includes four customizable home screen widgets with Material 3 dynamic theming.

Features

  • Google Calendar, Tasks, and Contacts birthday sync
  • Four widget styles: Dynamic, Simple, Month, and Flip
  • Material 3 Expressive design with dynamic wallpaper theming
  • Light, Dark, and AMOLED display modes
  • Month list bird's-eye view
  • Customizable notifications with snooze
  • Premium: custom color themes, widget transparency, ad-free experience

How We Use Your Data

UltraCalendar accesses your Google Calendar events, Tasks, and Contacts birthdays solely to display them within the app and widgets. All data is stored locally on your device. We never transmit your personal data to external servers. No Google user data is used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the app's calendar functionality.

For complete details, please read our Privacy Policy.
